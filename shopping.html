Shopping Policies

Our commitment to transparency and customer satisfaction

Shipping Policy

We strive to process and ship all orders within 1-2 business days of receiving them. Shipping times may vary depending on your location and the shipping method selected.

Free standard shipping on all orders over $50!

Shipping Options:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days ($5.99 or free for orders over $50)
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days ($12.99)
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day ($24.99)

Please note that we do not ship on weekends or holidays. During peak seasons, processing times may be slightly longer.

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information.

Return & Exchange Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you're not happy with your order, we accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of delivery.

Return Conditions:

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached
  • Proof of purchase is required
  • Final sale items cannot be returned or exchanged
  • Customer is responsible for return shipping costs

How to Return:

  1. Contact our customer service team to initiate your return
  2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  3. Include the original packing slip or order confirmation
  4. Ship the package to our return center

Refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days of receiving your return. Please allow 1-2 billing cycles for the refund to appear on your statement.

Privacy Policy

At ThreadCraft, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address) when you place an order
  • Payment information processed securely through our payment gateway
  • Browsing behavior and preferences to improve your shopping experience

How We Use Your Information:

  • To process and fulfill your orders
  • To communicate with you about your order status
  • To improve our products and services
  • To send marketing communications (only with your consent)

We will never sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Additional customs fees may apply.

How do I contact customer service?

Our customer service team is available Monday-Friday, 9am-6pm EST. You can reach us by email at support@threadcraft.com or by phone at (555) 123-4567.

What is your sizing like?

Our t-shirts follow standard US sizing. We provide detailed size charts for each product to help you find the perfect fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up.
